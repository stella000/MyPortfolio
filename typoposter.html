Stella Ding

Creative Typography Poster

Typography, Graphic Design, Photography

Get started

TASK

The assignment is to choose a line from a song and develop original display letterforms to represent the characters. The name of the artist should be minimized but included on the poster.

I chose the line "you are my sweetest downfall" as my lyrics to work with. The song is about how a girl still loves the guy even if she get betrayed on. So the message is about the despair in love.

PROBLEM

The challenge of the assignment is to both generate letterforms that are interesting, unique, readable, and to compose a scene that go with the letterforms in order to deliver the message of the song.

PROCESS

I had the idea of using wax to compose the letters. I broke the wax into small pieces at first and immerse them in food colored water.

I tried to use different materials to compose the letters and set up a little scene with the flower and the table cloth. But the execution of the idea still needs to be refined. So I played more with it.

I tried to make the composition more interesting by make it more 3 dimensional. But then the some parts of the lyrics become hard to read.

I decided to go with the melted wax. It does look a lot more clean and also gets the message "still love" very well since it stays on the surface like staying on someone's heart.

END PRODUCT

FINAL THOUGHTS

If I have a chance to further work on this project, I will first experiment more with the food coloring to make sure the wine color is more accurate and play with the scene more.

I was thrilled at how powerful and unique types can become with my creativity. I am more than amazed at how types can become a form of art even to arouse emotions and communicate meanings.

Instructor Annabelle Gould
