About me

User Experience Designer

I am currently a junior at University of Washington studying Human Centered Design Engineering.

I think designers who only do design are not the best designers. I believe all I learn from everything I do eventually contributes to how I design because design is holistic and comprehensive.That is why I have been taking a variety of roles in marketing, research, event planning and more in order to deliver truly interconnected design.

I also strive to spread design thinking in all industries and discipline. Design as eventually problem solving, is powerful in everything we do. With that vision, I relaunched Pixel Husky, a club established by a design senior Chip, and extend the club to connect the non-designers with the design community in order for promoting better understanding towards design.

This portfolio has a collection of work, but not a collection of my passion! Let's talk!
