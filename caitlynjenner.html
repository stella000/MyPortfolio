Caitlyn Jenner Logo Design

Typography, Graphic Design

Get started

TASK

I need to design a single letterform logo that represents key character attributes of your assigned person which for me is Caiylyn Jenner. I have to use their first or last initial as my letter.

PROBLEM

The challenge of the Caitlyn Jenner logo is to use the shape, color and pattern of the letter itself to represent the person with my own interpretation.

IDEA BEHIND THE LETTER

I used the transition from blue sans serif to pink serif to demonstrate her gender transition. I also add in my own interpretation by making the letter looks like an impossible letter to show her ambiguity because she seems reluctant to help the LGBT community despite the fact that she is expecting everyone to accept her new gender identity.

Instructor Annabelle Gould

Date Winter 2016

Duration 1 week
