CAITLYN JENNER LETTER DESIGN

I need to design a single letterform logo that represents key character attributes of Caiylyn Jenner using their first or last initial as my letter.

I used the transition from blue sans serif to pink serif to demonstrate her gender transition.

I also add in my own interpretation by making the letter looks like an impossible letter to show her ambiguity because she seems reluctant to help the LGBT community despite the fact that she is expecting everyone to accept her new gender identity.

TYPOGRAPHY POSTER

I need to choose a line from a song and develop original display letterforms to represent the characters. The name of the artist should be minimized but included on the poster.

I chose the line "you are my sweetest downfall" as my lyrics to work with. The song is about how a girl still loves the guy even if she get betrayed on. So the message is about the despair in love.
